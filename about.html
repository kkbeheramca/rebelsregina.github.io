About

A brief history of about us

Rebels Regina - former name SaskSuperStars is the right place to start your cricketing experiance

SaskSuperStars Cricket Club was established on Jan 2016. We played BigBash League and RCA league with a total of 18 games in the summer of 2016. We also had plenty of indoor sessions in the winter of 2016.

By the begining of Jan 2017 we were asked by Regina Cricket Association to change the name of the club as there were name conflit which RCA did not notice during the 2016 league. By the end of Jan 2017 we renamed our club to Rebels Regina Cricket Club. We are looking forward to start the 2017 league under new name and new colour.

Your Guarantees

What you can expect by joining our club

Rebels Regina Cricket Club is a thriving home for cricketers of all abilities We offer all visitors and indeed prospective new members a warm welcome. We provide lessons and programs which are overseen by some of the India’s state level players and experts.

Access to the sport is available to virtually all members, with juniors as young as seven able to participate. Our sides accommodate cricketers young and old, from a thriving junior section, through “leisure” cricketers to keen club cricketers playing league cricket at varying levels.We look forward to an exciting season in 2016 and a good spring to benefit ground preparation, with the club challenging for honours in both leagues, as we continue to give players the chance to develop and improve. The theme of development is best demonstrated by our thriving junior section developing our future senior players.

What our current member enjoy

We guarentee all our players will get the chance to play in almost all the league matches. Though the position in the team is based on performance we make sure every intrested player gets a position in the team. All our members can participate in our indoor tournaments. We have the facility and resources which can be made use by our members. There will beatleast two hours of game in every two week which all our intrested members can make use of.
Rebels Regina members can also participate in all the social events organised by the club and also can make use of the kits whenever needed by the players. Our member can enjoy the summer to its full by making use of the facilites avilable.
